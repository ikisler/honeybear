About

Honey Bear Restaurant is a classic American eatery with some European details. We believe in offering food made from only the freshest ingredients, sourced as locally as possible. We do this because we believe that the freshness of our ingredients directly effects the taste of our food, as well as the importance of supporting the local economy.
